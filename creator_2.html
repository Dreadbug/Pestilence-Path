What is your primary occupation?

Farmer (Hard): Start with 200 marc of food, but 500 pf and earn 2x points
Barber (Normal): Start with 1250 pf and earn 1x points
Doctor (Easy): Start with 2000 pf and 50 marc of herbs, but earn 0.25x points

When would you like to leave?

Winter (Hard): Bitter cold conditions and a lack of food keep your daily travels short
Spring (Easy): Warmer weather and more available food, but greater risk of getting stuck or injured
Summer (Medium): Scorching heat but a budding harvest and longer periods of daylight to travel
Fall (Very Easy): Cool, fair weather and an abundant harvest to keep you going
